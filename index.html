about
I am a data scientist with a background in pure mathematics, finance, and engineering. I am currently a postdoc at dtu and the copenhagen center for social data science, and a guest researcher at danmarks statistik. I am also co-founder of mirand.ai, applying AI and computer vision to large-scale fashion datasets.

My research is in computational social science — combining large-scale human behavior datasets with statistical and machine learning methods. I have published in pnas, nature human behavior, and machine learning conferences (iclr, aistats).
